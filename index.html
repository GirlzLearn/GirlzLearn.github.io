Why Girlz Learn?

Girlz Learn is an educational site that provides an insight on various prevalent topics. This website uniquely strays from other sites because it is specifically designed to appeal to school students; relatable examples with complete step by step instructions (from the CS courses) and upcoming engaging lessons for Economics and Personal Finance that are not seen elsewhere. As an avid programmer and having keen interest in the fields of economics and finance, I feel this the perfect mix of courses/lessons that readily prepare the younger generation for the future and opens the door for many more opportunities to come.
